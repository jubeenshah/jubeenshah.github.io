# KitKat 12-Ton Heist — The Complete Brand Response Archive

In late March 2026, KitKat (Nestlé) confirmed that 12 tonnes of KitKat products were stolen in transit between their factory in Central Italy and their destination in Poland. What followed was one of the largest spontaneous brand bandwagon moments in social media history — dozens of brands across industries and geographies issued mock "Official Statements" on Instagram, each playfully self-incriminating while promoting their own products.

- **This document catalogs 120+ brand responses collected from Instagram.**

---

- **Handle:** [@kitkat](https://www.instagram.com/kitkat) · **Engagement:** 12K reposts
- **Statement:**
We can confirm that 12T of KitKat products were stolen while in transit between our factory in Central Italy and their destination in Poland. We are working closely with local authorities and supply chain partners to investigate. The good news: there are no concerns for consumer safety, and supply is not affected. Thank you.
